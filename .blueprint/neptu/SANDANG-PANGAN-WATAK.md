# Sandang Pangan & Primbon Watak Reference

Traditional Balinese astrology reference data for prosperity forecasting and personality traits by age.

## Sandang Pangan Dalam 6 Tahun (Prosperity in Six Years)

Prosperity levels based on **total_urip** (sum of sapta_wara + panca_wara + sad_wara urip) and age ranges in 6-year intervals.

### Prosperity Level Codes

| Code | Indonesian                            | English                             |
| ---- | ------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| 0    | Kesakitan (penderitaan)               | Poor, health condition              |
| 1    | Penghasilan sedikit                   | Minimum income                      |
| 2    | Di tengah (penghasilan sedang)        | Medium income                       |
| 3    | Baik (penghasilan baik)               | Good income                         |
| 4    | Baik sekali (penghasilan baik sekali) | Very good income                    |
| 5    | Hidup senang (penghasilan baik)       | Happy life (good income)            |
| 7    | Paripurna (hidup mewah)               | Luxurious life                      |
| 8    | Hidup mewah (semua tercapai)          | Luxurious life, everything achieved |

## Primbon Watak (Sifat) Menurut Umur — Personality by Age

Character traits determined by the person's **total_urip modulo 12** (1-12 cycle). Each group has ages at 12-year intervals.

### Watak 01 — Ages: 01, 13, 25, 37, 49, 61, 73, 85, 97

**ID:** Kemanja-manjaan, hati terasa terang, bermunculan firasat-firasat, kadang-kadang berlaku nekat, bahayanya kena fitnah.

**EN:** This person is pampering, has clear heart, intuitive, occasionally does anything no matter what would happen, he is also at risk of being slandered.

### Watak 02 — Ages: 02, 14, 26, 38, 50, 62, 74, 86, 98

**ID:** Keras hati, angkuh, ingin berkuasa, usahanya mendapat dukungan dari orang yang berpangkat, kariernya menanjak, namun mudah dimanfaatkan oleh orang dengan maksud tertentu. Patut waspada agar jangan menyesal dikemudian hari. Bersifat kuat. Bahayanya terhadap benda tajam.

**EN:** This person has strong determination, arrogant, is interested in power gain, his efforts enjoy support from important people. This person has good career but can easily be manipulated by others for specific purposes. This person need to be careful to avoid regret in the future. His strong. Avoid weapons such knives etc.

### Watak 03 — Ages: 03, 15, 27, 39, 51, 63, 75, 87, 99

**ID:** Mudah bingung, sukar berkonsentrasi. Terasa kehilangan gairah hidup. Kemauan kerja muncul bila sudah terdesak oleh keluarga, terutama kebutuhan ekonomi. Sering kena tipu. Mudah diserang sakit kepala, batuk-batuk dan sakit dada.

**EN:** This person is easily confused, difficult to concentrate and lose spirit. His willingness to work emerges due to family economic needs. His frequently cheated, easily suffers from headaches, coughs and chest pains.

### Watak 04 — Ages: 04, 16, 28, 40, 52, 64, 76, 88, 100

**ID:** Selalu waspada hemat, suka menabung. Nampak kikir dan rakus karena suka mengumpulkan uang (menabung) demi masa depannya serta keluarga. Mudah sakit pada pencernaan. Harus pandai mengikuti arus, situasi, bila tidak akan membahayakan.

**EN:** This person is always economical and likes saving. He looks stingy and greedy because he likes saving his future and family. He can easily suffer from digestion problems. He should be careful in anticipating situation otherwise, he could be in great danger.

### Watak 05 — Ages: 05, 17, 29, 41, 53, 65, 77, 89, 101

**ID:** Ramah, suka bergurau, percaya diri. Gampang mendapat kemudahan (fasilitas), keinginannya cepat terwujud, karena mudah pula menjadi tamak. Hendaknya hati-hati mengatur keuangan, sebab kemudahan itu tidak selalu ada. Waspadalah terhadap godaan pria / wanita, sebab akan ada godaan yang menghalang.

**EN:** He is friendly, like joking and self confident. This person has easy access to facilities, and his wishes can easily be realised making him greedy. He should be careful in managing money as such facilities are not always available. He should be careful with men's or women's temptation – it is just around the corner.

### Watak 06 — Ages: 06, 18, 30, 42, 54, 66, 78, 90, 102

**ID:** Suka mencampuri urusan orang lain, sombong. Tidak menyadari perubahan usia, sehingga dia merasa seperti yang dulu. Mudah terserang penyakit mata, mudah terganggu godaan sexual. Hati-hati terhadap musuh dalam selimut, sering menerima surat kaleng.

**EN:** This person is arrogant and like interfering with other people's business. He does not realise his age status making him just like the way he was. He can easily suffer from eye problems, or sexual seduction which is very close to this person.

### Watak 07 — Ages: 07, 19, 31, 43, 55, 67, 79, 91, 103

**ID:** Pada jenjang ini rasa cemburu bermunculan, ia peramah, disukai orang. Dekat dengan atasannya. Mudah naik pangkat, bila berwiraswasta banyak relasinya. Bertanggung jawab terhadap tugas dan kewajibannya. Kesehatan cukup baik, hati-hati bila memanjat pohon.

**EN:** At this stage this person is very jealous but he's friendly and well liked by others. He is close to his superior and will have a lot of contacts and relations if he goes into business. He is responsible for his assigned duties and responsibility. This person is in good health condition, but should be careful when climbing up a tree.

### Watak 08 — Ages: 08, 20, 32, 44, 56, 68, 80, 92, 104

**ID:** Suka menyendiri, kata-katanya lembut menyejukkan hati, teguh pendirian, walaupun dirundung kesedihan. Rejekinya banyak tapi lekas habis karena ia pemurah hati / suka menolong orang. Ia harus berusaha keras untuk menutupi biaya-biaya hidupnya, sering pindah tempat karena gangguan-gangguan yang muncul. Jaga kesehatan paru-paru. Untuk mengatasi keresahan hati, hendaknya ingat melakukan sembahyang leluhur.

**EN:** This person likes being alone, his voice is tender and soft. He is persistent in spite of all sorrows. He can easily obtain his luck but he spends money quite easily too because he likes helping people. He should work hard to cover all expenses and moves from one place to another due to frequent problems. He should take good care of his lung. He needs to pray for his ancestors in order to get rid of his nervousness.

### Watak 09 — Ages: 09, 21, 33, 45, 57, 69, 81, 93, 105

**ID:** Muncul keragu-raguan, namun kalau terpaksa bisa jadi nekat, suka keluar rumah, untuk mencari informasi, demi kesejahteraan bawahannya maupun keluarga, sering berpergian dan membawa keberhasilan, namun godaan-godaan selalu menghadangnya. Jangan kerja jadi pengemudi, sebab watak ragu-ragu akan membahayakannya. Kesehatan terganggu karena pusing, penyakit perut dan penyakit dalam berpergian.

**EN:** This person is doubtful but he could also act in a determined way if he has to. He enjoys going out for information for the sake of his subordinates and family. He frequently travels and comes back with success but he is also faced with hindrances. He should not work as a driver because his doubt can be dangerous. His health problems will be headaches, stomachache and other travel-related illness.

### Watak 10 — Ages: 10, 22, 34, 46, 58, 70, 82, 94, 106

**ID:** Bijaksana, ilmuan, berarti utama. Tidak suka terkekang, tidak mudah didikte. Usahanya demi cita-cita akan terwujud, namun ia nampak kurang teganya bila terikat kepada keluarganya. Kesehatan jaga penyakit jantung, paru-paru. Jangan ingkar janji membahayakan.

**EN:** He is while knowledge and science mean a lot to him. This person likes freedom and cannot be dictated. He does not seem to enjoy being detached from family. For his health concern, he should be careful with heart attack and lung problems. He should keep his promise, as it could be dangerous otherwise.

### Watak 11 — Ages: 11, 23, 35, 47, 59, 71, 83, 95, 107

**ID:** Tenang, namun penuh disiplin. Bersikap pasif, senang dirumah, rejekinya pas-pasan namun tetap merasa puas. Mudah diserang penyakit rematik. Orang iri hati bila ia berhasil bahkan dimusuhi, namun dapat menyelamatkan diri.

**EN:** He is calm but well disciplined, passive and enjoys staying at home. He is happy although what he has is just enough for the day. He should be careful with rheumatism. People might be jealous for his success, he will survive.

### Watak 12 — Ages: 12, 24, 36, 48, 60, 72, 84, 96, 108

**ID:** Cemburu, keras hati tetapi welas asih dan sosial, berdisiplin. Pada usia ini bagaikan berada dipersimpangan jalan. Harus waspada untuk menentukan langkah yang tepat dan bijaksana adalah pasrah kepada Tuhan Yang Maha Esa. Bila salah pilih anda bisa terjerumus oleh rayuan, hawa nafsu dan bisa dipengaruhi oleh roh-roh jahat juga berusaha untuk mempengaruhi manusia. Iblis suka merayu dan menjanjikan kesenangan duniawi, yang semu sifatnya, inilah yang membawa kejalan yang sesat.

**EN:** He jealous, self-determined but kind hearted and self-disciplined. At his current age he is like going into an intersection. He should careful in making his decision regarding which way to take. The wise and good decision would be to surrender himself to the supreme being. If he made a wrong choice he could be influenced by bad spirit or controlled by his ego. Satan like to promise us with material wealth which will bring this person to the world of darkness.
